How To Play ?
This Project is in Beta. Use at your own risk

How to play:

  1. Opening price of 4 hour candle of BTC at 4 PM UTC will decide the winner of the session.
  2. To participate you need to select the slot under which you want to play according to your risk appetite.
  3. Now you need to click on participate. After that now need to predict the price of BTC .
  4. After this approve and submit the prediction.
  5. All the participants can see the predictions placed by other participant when the slot closed down for prediction. This is to ensure fairness in the game.
  6. If more than one participant predict the same price then winner will be selected randomly with the help of lottery software.
  7. Participants can check the history of each game.
